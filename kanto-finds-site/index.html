More About Kanto Finds

Kanto Finds is a locator to find every places in the Philippines including barangays, stores, pharmacies and more. This was founded by a pair student developers. When things get confusing of finding barangays and every stores, nothing to worry, this were the ideas are getting started to solve everything.

Mission

To make it easier to use access for the people locating every places like pharmacy, snack stores, etc. For more information to see anytime, anywhere.


Vision

To make solutions and connections for the people and local communities across in the Cabanatuan City, Nueva Ecija, Philippines.
